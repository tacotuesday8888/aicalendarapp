Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

AI Efficiency is a student planning app for goals, calendar planning, study sessions, reflections, syllabus imports, subscriptions, and AI-assisted planning.

Information We Collect

When you use the app, we may collect account information such as your email address, app content you create such as goals and planner blocks, imported syllabus text or files you choose to provide, usage events, device diagnostics, and subscription status.

How We Use Information

We use this information to provide the app, sync your data, run AI-assisted planning features, process imports, manage subscriptions, improve reliability, prevent abuse, and support account export or deletion requests.

AI Features

AI features may send relevant app context, prompts, goals, planner content, or imported syllabus content to backend services and AI providers so the requested feature can generate a response. Do not submit sensitive information unless it is needed for your planning workflow.

Third-Party Services

The app may use service providers such as Firebase, Google Cloud, RevenueCat, Apple, Google Sign-In, and AI model providers. These services process information as needed to provide authentication, storage, backend functions, purchases, diagnostics, and AI features.

Data Export and Deletion

You can request export or deletion of your app data from within the app when those features are available. Some records may be retained as required for security, legal, billing, or abuse-prevention reasons.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app developer through the support channel listed in the App Store or in the app.
