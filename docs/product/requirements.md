# Product Requirements

## Product scope

`aicalendarapp` is an iPhone-first student planning product centered on Today planning, goals, scheduling, study sessions, reflections, reminders, and a context-aware AI assistant.

## Core user journeys

- Sign up, sign in, and restore access
- Complete onboarding and initial setup
- Connect Apple Calendar and optionally import a syllabus
- Create, edit, reorder, complete, and delete goals
- Run study sessions with notes and attachments
- Submit morning, midday, night, and quick vibe check-ins
- Ask the assistant for planning help and explicitly confirm draft actions
- Purchase or restore a subscription
- Manage account data and request deletion/export

## Premium scope

Premium value is concentrated in AI planning, assistant help, advanced imports, and planner acceleration flows. Basic account and planning functionality stays available without AI-generated automation.
